Quality

Food safety and continuous improvement

Our quality policy applies measurable standards across all processes for food safety, traceability and meeting customer specifications.

Core principles

  • Raw material acceptance and supplier evaluation
  • Hygiene and critical control points on production lines
  • Batch/lot traceability
  • Pack integrity and label accuracy checks
  • Complaint, recall and corrective action procedures
Commitment Regulatory compliance and adherence to agreed product specifications.
